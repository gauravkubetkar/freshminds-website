Physical Health

Cardiovascular

Cardiovascular exercises,
elevate heart rate and improve circulation, promoting overall
cardiovascular health and endurance. They're essential for enhancing lung capacity and reducing the risk of heart disease,
fostering vitality and stamina.

Strength Training

Strength training, such as weightlifting
and bodyweight exercises,
builds muscle, bone density, and metabolism,
promoting better movement and reducing injury risk.

CrossFit

CrossFit is a dynamic fitness program aerobic exercises,
and gymnastics to enhance strength, endurance,
and overall fitness through high-intensity functional movements.

Yoga

Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga integrates physical postures and breath control
to achieve balance, strength, and relaxation,
fostering harmony between body and mind.

Raja Yoga

Raja Yoga focuses on meditation and self-discipline,
aiming to attain spiritual
enlightenment and inner peace through
mental concentration and contemplation.

Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga focuses on coordinated
breath and movement through a set
sequence of poses, fostering physical strength,
flexibility, and mental clarity.
